About the Content

Each section of this page is marked by headings of different levels. This helps organize the information hierarchically and makes it easier to read. HTML provides six levels of headings, from <h1> to <h6>.

Importance of Headings

Headings are important for both accessibility and SEO. They help screen readers navigate the content and allow search engines to better understand the structure of your page.
